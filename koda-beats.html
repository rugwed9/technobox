QUICK START

1. Press 3 for Fred Again (or 4 for Charlotte, etc.)
2. Press Q W E to start kick + hat + snare
3. Press T for bass, Y for lead
4. Drag the XY pad for filter sweeps
5. Hold Z to filter down, release for the drop
6. Hit A for riser, then S for drop impact
7. Hold . for sidechain pump
8. Click sequencer grid to edit patterns
9. ESC kills everything
